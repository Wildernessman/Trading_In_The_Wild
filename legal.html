Tax Considerations

Trading Income

Income from trading activities may be subject to taxation. The specific tax implications can vary depending on your location, the type of trading you engage in, and your individual circumstances.

Capital Gains

Profits from trading may be subject to capital gains tax. The rate and method of calculation can vary based on factors such as holding period and your tax jurisdiction.

Record Keeping

Maintaining accurate records of your trades is crucial for tax reporting purposes. We recommend keeping detailed logs of all your trading activities.
